About Me

I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Creative Media, with a concentration in Video Game Design and Development, at the University of Hawaii - West Oahu (UHWO). I am expected to graduate in Spring 2025. My education allows me to blend my passion for technology and creativity, equipping me with the skills to develop engaging and interactive content. My journey into coding began when I was introduced to the field by a friend pursuing a programming career. Intrigued by the idea, I dove into research and began creating personal projects. Over the past 6-7 months, during the pandemic, I've been self-teaching through online resources like FreeCodeCamp and W3Schools. Through Massive Open Online Courses (MOOC), I’ve familiarized myself with languages and libraries such as JavaScript, Bootstrap, and JQuery.
